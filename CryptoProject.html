Cryptocurrency: Price and Makret

What is Crytpcurrency?

Crytpocurrency is a type of currency which doesn’t rely on bank to verify transactions, instead its a peer-to-peer system that allows us to send and receive payments from anyone to anywhere. It runs on a blockchain, which is a record of all transactions updated and held by the currency holders.

Purpose

Goal : To find correlations between cyrptocurrency market capitalization, prices and 24hr volumes to indentify potential investment opportunities. Lets understand the terms in brief:

  • Market Capitalization: It shows how big or valuable a coin is, thus to decide if its worth buying. This is done via comparing the current value of the coin and all other available units combined based on supply and demand forces at work in CryptoCurrency eco system.

  • Prices: Price of each coin trading at any given time.

  • 24h volume: Volume of the coin traded in last 24h. This helps users to understand the momentum of the market vis-a-vis volume traded during that period for cryptocurrency trading and understanding liquidity situation in any given period.

Key Factors: The worth of the coin and future of most and least worthy coin.
